# LLM Wiki

## What It Is

An LLM Wiki is a maintained markdown knowledge base that sits between raw source documents and user queries. The LLM incrementally updates it as new information arrives.

## Why It Matters

It turns one-off retrieval into accumulation. Instead of recomputing synthesis from scratch on every question, the system preserves prior structure, links, summaries, and contradictions.

## Key Details

- The wiki is persistent and compounding.
- The LLM is responsible for writing, updating, and cross-linking pages.
- Raw sources remain separate and immutable.
- The schema document constrains the LLM into repeatable maintenance behavior.
