# Persistent Synthesis

## What It Is

Persistent synthesis is the practice of compiling knowledge into durable wiki pages that are revised over time, rather than regenerated from retrieved raw fragments for every query.

## Why It Matters

This is the main advantage of the LLM Wiki pattern. It reduces repeated rediscovery work, preserves previous reasoning, and makes cross-document insights available immediately for later questions.

## Key Details

- Contradictions can be recorded once and reused.
- Cross-references accumulate over time.
- Answers to good questions can become future source material inside the wiki.
- The value of the wiki increases as more sources are integrated.
