# Obsidian

## Overview

Obsidian is the local markdown environment used to browse, inspect, and navigate the wiki maintained by the LLM.

## Relevance

The seed source frames Obsidian as the IDE for the second brain: the user explores the graph, reads pages, follows links, and watches the wiki evolve while the LLM performs the maintenance work.
